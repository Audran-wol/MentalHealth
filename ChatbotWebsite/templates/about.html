About Us

Greetings from our mental health chatbot! This platform is crafted to offer dependable insights and support aimed at enhancing your mental health and wellness.

We recognize that reaching out for mental health support can be difficult, so our chatbot is created to ensure the experience is as easy and welcoming as possible.

Please note that this chatbot is part of a school project and is not intended for professional use. If you are facing a mental health crisis, it is important to seek assistance from a qualified mental health professional.
